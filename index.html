Our History

Kafui Tot Centre was established to provide quality early childhood education and holistic development opportunities for children between the ages of two to six years at Dedenya Shaihills in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana.

Recognizing the critical importance of the early years in children's growth and future success, KTC creates a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can develop academically.

Since the beginning, KTC has been committed to supporting children in underserved communities. Through dedicated teaching, community engagement, and child-centered learning approaches, the center continues to help lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning and development.

Vision

To be a leading early childhood education centre in underserved communities that transforms lives by giving every child a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide accessible high-quality early childhood education that nurtures the intellectual, social, emotional, physical, and moral development of every child in underserved communities, empowering them to reach their full potential and become responsible members of society.

Core Values

  • Child-centered learning: We place the needs, interest, and development of every child at the heart of everything we do.
  • Inclusiveness: We believe every child deserves access to quality education regardless of background or circumstances.
  • Community partnership: We work collaboratively with parents, caregivers, volunteers, and donors.
  • Innovation: We embrace creative approaches and continuous improvement in teaching.
  • Excellence: We thrive for the highest standards in education, care, and service delivery.

The Founder's Story

"Why should some children have access to safe classrooms, qualified teachers, books, and opportunities, while others are forced to overcome enormous obstacles just to receive a basic education?"

For the founder of KTC Center, the struggle for education began long before she entered a classroom. She grew up in a rural village where access to basic necessities such as clean water and electricity was nonexistent, and where quality education was not within reach. Attending class meant walking long distances along isolated paths.

Today, KTC exists because one girl who once walked those difficult roads refused to accept that this should be the reality for another generation of children. Kafui Tot Center is more than an educational initiative; it is a commitment to children who have been overlooked, underserved, and left behind.

Because education is not a privilege reserved for a fortunate few. It is a fundamental right.

Deborah Kafui Manye Founder and Executive Director
